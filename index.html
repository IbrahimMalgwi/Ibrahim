About Me

Software Engineer

Ibrahim Gana Malgwi is a Backend Software Engineer specializing in Java, Python, and distributed backend architectures. With over five years of experience, he has designed and delivered enterprise‑grade systems across digital payments, financial services, and healthcare interoperability. His CV highlights “strong specialization in Java, Python, JavaScript… and distributed system architectures,” which forms the backbone of his engineering approach. Ibrahim builds secure, scalable backend services using Spring Boot, FastAPI, Django, and modern microservices patterns. He is highly experienced with HL7 FHIR, OpenHIE, and DHIS2 integrations, and has engineered event‑driven systems powered by Kafka, Redis, and RabbitMQ. He currently works at AppGlobal Technologies, where he leads backend development for healthcare and payment platforms — designing FHIR‑compliant APIs, integrating financial systems, and deploying cloud‑native services using Docker, Kubernetes, and CI/CD automation. Beyond coding, Ibrahim enjoys mentoring engineers, improving engineering processes, and collaborating with cross‑functional teams to deliver reliable, compliant, and high‑performance solutions. He is passionate about solving real‑world problems with technology and continues to explore innovations in cloud engineering, distributed systems, and secure software design.
